First iteration of the frame

Our first design didn’t work out too well. As we attached foam to the frame. it would stretch the elastic bands until a point where it wasn’t snug enough to support all that weight. Top of the frame wasn’t solid enough to support large ears which would also become a problem. We were able to go with an ad-hoc solution and extend the existing frame with two thicker elastic bands going over the top of the head. »
